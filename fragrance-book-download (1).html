✅ Universal Layering Rules
1. Always layer: lotion → oil → mist → EDP/perfume
2. Moisturized warm skin holds scent 2-3x longer — never skip lotion
3. Pulse points: wrists, neck, collarbone, inner elbow, behind knees
4. Never rub wrists — it breaks fragrance molecules and kills the dry-down
5. Hair holds scent for hours — always finish with a mist through hair
6. On warm skin: use 1-2 fewer sprays in summer — heat amplifies everything
7. Same-family notes always layer safely
8. Oils underneath light scents = the #1 trick for warm-skin longevity
